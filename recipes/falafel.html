Falafel

Description

Tasty falafels!

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Garlic and fresh herbs
  • Dried spices
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Soak your chickpeas. Add dried chickpeas to a bowl, cover with several inches of water, and leave it overnight. They will triple in size and be ready in the morning.
  2. The next day, you will toss the soaked chickpeas and other falafel ingredients into a food processor and pulse until the mixture is chopped small, stopping before it purees into a paste.
  3. Chill the mixture for 15 minutes, then form into balls. I usually get around 18 falafel balls from this recipe. You can chill again or move on to cooking them.
  4. For the crispiest, most authentic falafel, fry them in oil.
